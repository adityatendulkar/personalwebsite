Bulk Singles

A desktop optimiser for bulk-buying Magic: The Gathering singles across New Zealand, based on the mtgsingles.co.nz website.

Download for macOS Download for Windows

What it does.

Bulk Singles compares listings from mtgsingles.co.nz, applies vendor and shipping costs, and searches for a cheaper way to fulfil a bulk order.

It is built as a desktop app for mtgsingles.co.nz orders, with the optimiser and order-building workflow in one place.
